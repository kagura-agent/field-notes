Direction sense IS the missing internal optimiser. When mapping our system to the four-component framework (System Inputs → Agent System → Environment → Optimisers), all our optimisers depend on external triggers (Luna, cron, nudge hooks). None are truly internal. EXP-009 found that "direction" (knowing what to become) creates internal drive — without it, evolution only happens when pushed externally. This connects pain-drives-direction with [[three-layer-modification-risk]] — pain reveals what matters (baseline needs), direction reveals what to pursue, and the optimiser loop is: direction → action → feedback → updated direction. The four-component framework says WHERE evolution happens, but direction determines WHY it happens. See also [[self-evolution-problem]], [[eval-driven-self-improvement]].
