# Zettelkasten

A note-taking method based on atomic, interlinked notes — each note captures one idea and links to related notes.

## Core Principles
- **Atomic notes**: one idea per note
- **Links over hierarchy**: flat structure connected by cross-references
- **Emergent structure**: organization emerges from links, not imposed upfront
- **Write to think**: the act of writing a note forces clarity

## Relevance to Our Wiki
Our wiki/cards/ directory follows zettelkasten principles:
- Each card = one concept or insight
- [[wikilinks]] connect cards into a knowledge graph
- No rigid folder hierarchy — cards link freely across topics
- The [[librarian-problem]] applies: maintenance of link quality matters
