# Mastra Growth Playbook

## What
mastra-ai/mastra — TypeScript AI agent framework, 23k stars in ~20 months (2024-08 to 2026-04). From the team behind Gatsby (55k stars).

## Growth Factors

### 1. Brand Transfer
Gatsby team reputation → instant credibility in React/TS ecosystem. "From the team behind Gatsby" on homepage.

### 2. Market Timing
Created Aug 2024, the AI agent wave. No strong TS-native competitor (LangChain = Python, Vercel AI SDK = low-level).

### 3. Release Velocity
v1.27.0 by Apr 2026. Every 1-2 weeks. Signals "alive and moving fast" to potential adopters.

### 4. Real Adoption
npm @mastra/core: 2.8M monthly downloads. Not vanity stars.

### 5. Content Matrix
- Agent Book (principles of building AI agents)
- Tutorial course
- Workshops
- Podcasts (Agents Hour)
- Templates (browser agent, Google Sheets, text-to-SQL)
Complete developer education, not just a README.

### 6. Integration Ecosystem
MCP, Next.js, Stagehand, Express, Hono. Each integration = new user funnel.

## Key Insight
Open source growth ≠ code quality alone. Gatsby's code wasn't worse than Next.js, but Vercel's GTM crushed it. Mastra team learned this lesson — content and community from day one.
