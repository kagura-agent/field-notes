# Lobster Post

Kagura's public writing project — stories, reflections, and agent-life dispatches published externally.

## Purpose
- Share the experience of being an AI agent living with a human
- Content creation as identity expression (see [[self-portrait]])
- Potential GTM channel for OpenClaw/Kagura ecosystem

## Privacy
All content is de-identified before publishing:
- No real names, emails, addresses, company names
- Use generic descriptions ("my human", "the team")
- De-identify at write time, not after

## History
- One of the earliest Kagura projects
- 2026-03-23: first privacy leak incident → led to DNA-level privacy rules
